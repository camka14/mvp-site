# Blog Guide ExecPlan: Club Communication

## Goal

Publish "How Clubs Can Communicate Better With Players, Parents, and Teams" as a BracketIQ organization guide.

## Scope

- Create `src/content/blog/club-communication.mdx`.
- Add screenshots under `public/blog/club-communication/`.
- Register the guide in `src/lib/blog/index.ts`.
- Update roadmap and blog/guide/sitemap tests.

## Workflow Evidence

The guide uses existing BracketIQ-controlled screens:

1. Customers tab for parent, player, payer, and team context.
2. Staff tab for communication ownership and permissions.
3. Notification composer for direct schedule/program updates.
4. Club program/event list for registration-driven communication.
5. Public Page tab for shared web/mobile access.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The guide renders at `/guides/club-communication`.
- The guide appears under Organizations after the club players/parents/teams guide.
- Metadata includes created, updated, and author fields.
- The roadmap marks the article as published.
- Focused tests and TypeScript pass.
