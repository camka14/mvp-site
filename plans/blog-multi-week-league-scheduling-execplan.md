# Multi-Week League Scheduling Guide

## Goal

Create the BracketIQ guide `How to Schedule a Multi-Week Sports League` as the next dependency-safe League guide after league creation and management.

## Scope

- Use an indoor soccer league as the generic sport example.
- Show the real BracketIQ flow for configuring weekly availability, reviewing participants, rescheduling matches, checking Agenda view, and verifying the public page.
- Capture screenshots from the local app with Agenda view for schedule images.
- Register the guide under `/guides/multi-week-league-scheduling`.
- Update the roadmap and focused tests.

## Workflow

1. Seed a local article fixture with teams, fields, weekly timeslots, and matches.
2. Capture each guide step from the rendered app.
3. Write the end-user instructions against the screenshots.
4. Add blog registry metadata, guide nav order, sitemap expectations, and roadmap status.
5. Run focused blog/guide tests, typecheck, and browser smoke tests.
