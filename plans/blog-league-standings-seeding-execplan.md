# League Standings and Playoff Seeding Guide

## Goal

Create the BracketIQ guide `How to Manage League Standings and Playoff Seeding` as the next League guide after league creation, management, and multi-week scheduling.

## Scope

- Use an indoor soccer league as the generic sport example.
- Show the real BracketIQ workflow for reviewing scored matches, checking standings, adjusting final points, confirming results, and checking playoff seeding.
- Use Agenda view for schedule screenshots.
- Capture screenshots from the rendered local app.
- Register the guide under `/guides/league-standings-playoff-seeding`.
- Update the roadmap and focused tests.

## Workflow

1. Seed a local article fixture with a league, completed regular-season matches, standings, and playoff bracket placeholders.
2. Capture the guide steps from the rendered app.
3. Write the end-user instructions against those screenshots.
4. Add blog registry metadata, guide nav order, sitemap expectations, and roadmap status.
5. Run focused blog/guide tests, typecheck, and a rendered route smoke test.
